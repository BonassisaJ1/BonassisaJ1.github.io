Image Sensors

The Different Types of Sensors

URL Image Sensors Explained
Site Authors Jeff Meyer and Alex Summersby
Abstract Canon Europe explains the role of image sensors in taking photographs. It highlights the two types of sensors: CCD and CMOS. CCD sensors offer high-quality images with low noise but use more power, making them suitable for professional uses. CMOS sensors, on the other hand, are more power-efficient, allowing for faster processing, though they historically had lower image quality. The article also discusses the advancements in sensor technology, including increased resolution, dynamic range, and low-light performances. Overall, the article explains the importance of image sensors in modern photography, showcasing how they continue to evolve to meet the demands of both amateur and professional photographers.

Sizes of Sensors

URL The Different Sizes of Sensors
Site Author Dan Zafra
Abstract The article discusses the significance of camera sensor size in photography. It explains how sensor size affects image quality, depth of field, and low-light performance. In general, larger sensors capture more light, leading to better image quality and detail. The article compares various sensor sizes, including full-frame, APS-C, and micro four-thirds, highlighting their pros and cons.The article also emphasizes that factors like lens quality and camera settings also play important roles in achieving great images as well as sensor size. Overall, understanding sensor size helps photographers choose the necessary equipment for their needs, to get the best image quality there is.
