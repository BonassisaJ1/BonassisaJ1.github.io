The Different Types of Sensors

URL Image Sensors Explained
Site Authors Jeff Meyer and Alex Summersby
Abstract Canon Europe explains the role of image sensors in taking photographs. It highlights the two types of sensors: CCD and CMOS. CCD sensors offer high-quality images with low noise but consume more power, making them suitable for professional applications. CMOS sensors, on the other hand, are more power-efficient, allowing for faster processing, though they historically had lower image quality. The article also discusses the advancements in sensor technology, including increased resolution, dynamic range, and low-light performances. Overall, the article explains the importance of image sensors in modern photography, showcasing how they continue to evolve to meet the demands of both amateur and professional photographers.
